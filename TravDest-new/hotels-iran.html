Hotels in Tehran


Shahr Hotel

Tehran Parse Forked Road, Damavand Ave.
TEHRAN
IRAN
Shahr Hotel is located in the eastern part of Tehran, approximately 11 km from the city centre. Accommodations are equipped with cable TV, mini bar, internet access and en suite bathroom. The hotel offers a restaurant serving local and international dishes and room service is available. Other facilities and services include an indoor pool, Jacuzzi, sauna, steam room and a conference hall. The Golestan Palace, Azadi Tower, National Museum of Iran, Milad Tower and the Jamshidieh Park are some of the tourist destinations in the area. Mehrabad International Airport is 26 km and Imam Khomeini International Airport is approximately 61 km from the property.
The SHAHR HOTEL is a Tourist hotel. Located in Tehran area. Shopping is accessible by bus/taxi and the nightlife/restaurants are located in the hotel.

Additional Information:
Number of floors: 4
Number of rooms: 110

Nearest Airports:
Imam Khomeini International Airport - directions: via Persian Gulf Fwy/Tehran-Qom Fwy/Route 7 - 61 Km or 60 minutes away from the hotel
Mehrabad International Airport - directions: via Resalat Hwy or via Hakim Expy - 26 Km or 30 minutes away from the hotel



Homa Hotel

Homa Hotel is a five star property located in the hub of Tehran, within a few minute walk to Vanak bazaars and Vali-e-Asr Avenue. Accommodations feature air conditioning, satellite TV, in-room safe, complimentary tea and coffee making facilities, mini bar, wireless internet connection and bathroom with bathtub, shower, hairdryer, bathrobe and slippers. The hotel houses a convenient 24 hour on-site cafe and guests can also enjoy a wide selection of Iranian and international cuisine at the hotel's restaurant, as well as a daily breakfast. Recreational facilities include an outdoor swimming pool, fitness centre, sauna and Jacuzzi. Massage therapy is also available. For additional convenience, the hotel also offers laundry, concierge, room service, housekeeping and private parking. The Mehrabad International Airport is approximately 30 minute drive away while the Tehran Imam Khomeini International Airport is 36 km from the Homa Hotel.
The HOMA HOTEL is a Tourist hotel. Located in Tehran area. Shopping is right next door and the nightlife/restaurants are located in the hotel.

Additional Information:
Number of floors: 16
Number of rooms: 143

Nearest Airports:
Tehran Imam Khomeini International Airport - directions: via Persian Gulf Fwy/Tehran-Qom Fwy/Route 7 - 56 Km or 50 minutes away from the hotel
Mehrabad International Airport - directions: via Sheikh Fazlollah Nouri Expy - 14 Km or 30 minutes away from the hotel
