Greece Visa Requirements

  • Covering Letter
  • 3 recent passport size photographs (white background-non glossy paper).
  • Bank Statement of the last 6 months.
  • Original NTN
  • Copy of CNIC
  • AIG Insurance
  • Copy of credit card/s
  • Confirmed hotel reservation.
  • Copy of Ticket

    • Business Visa Requirements

  • Covering Letter of the firm.
  • Invitation letter- 1 copy to be faxed directly to the Embassy.
  • 3 recent passport size photographs (white background-non glossy paper)
  • Bank Statement of the last 6 months.
  • Original NTN, Chamber's Certificate, Import Export License and Company Registration.
  • Copy of CNIC
  • AIG Insurance
  • Copy of credit card/s
  • Confirmed hotel reservation.
  • Copy of Ticket
