Hotels in Dubai


MAYFAIR HOTEL

The Mayfair Hotel is centrally located in Deira within close proximity to the Clock Tower, Al Ghurair shopping mall, Old Harbour and the Dubai Creek Golf Course. Heading towards Al Maktoum Street, Dubai International Airport is only 15 minutes drive away from the hotel. Each of the 131 guestrooms and suites feature satellite TV, wireless internet access, electronic safe, direct dial telephone and private bathroom with shower. Non smoking rooms are also available. The hotel offers various restaurants and bars. Leisure facilities include an outdoor swimming pool, jacuzzi, sauna and a fully equipped health club. Major attractions in Dubai are easily accessible from the hotel, including Burj Khalifa, Dubai Mall, Dubai Museum and Jumeirah beach.
The MAYFAIR HOTEL is a Tourist hotel. Located in City Centre - Deira area. Shopping is accessible by bus/taxi and the nightlife/restaurants are located in the hotel.

ABC ARABIAN SUITES

The Arabian Suites hotel offers rooms and suites in the central Mankhool district of Dubai. Each accommodation contains a kitchen with refrigerator, microwave and washing machine, flat-screen satellite TV, WiFi connectivity, and a balcony. In addition suites feature a lounge room with DVD player. Guests can dine in the hotels all-day restaurant, which serves a variety of international cuisines. Leisure facilities include 2 outdoor swimming pools with terraces, 2 well-equipped fitness rooms, a sauna and a steam room. The Arabian Suites hotel is just a 20 minute drive from Dubai International Airport.
The ABC ARABIAN SUITES is a Tourist, All Suite, Modern, Business, Full Service hotel. Located in Bur Dubai area. Shopping is within walking distance and the nightlife/restaurants are located in the hotel.

Hotels in Abu Dhabi



JUMEIRAH AT ETIHAD TOWERS - HOTEL

Jumeirah at Etihad Towers is a 5-star hotel rising from beachside setting and overlooking the views of the Arabian Gulf and world famous Corniche. The luxurious rooms and suites features wireless internet access, widescreen interactive HD LED TV, ipod/iphone docking station, individually controlled air conditioning, spacious bathroom with hair dryer and amenities, ironing facility, mini bar, coffee and tea making facility and in-room security safe. Guests can choose from various dining options at a number of in-house restaurants and lounge bars, where light snacks to fine dining and a wide selection of alcoholic and non-alcoholic beverages are available. Hotel facilities include swimming pools with poolside bar, private beach, fitness center with modern equipment and spa that offers a new generation of treatments. The hotel is 38 km away from Abu Dhabi International Airport and 165 km from Dubai International Airport.
The JUMEIRAH AT ETIHAD TOWERS - HOTEL is a Tourist, Business, Leisure hotel. Located in City Centre - Corniche area. Shopping is accessible by bus/taxi and the nightlife/restaurants are located in the hotel.

HILTON CAPITAL GRAND ABUDHABI

Conveniently situated in the heart of Abu Dhabi, the Al Maqta Hotel Abu Dhabi is within easy reach of the airport, Abu Dhabi national exhibition centre, and the city's key attractions. This five star hotel features a unique curved-glass design. Each room and suite features a flat-screen TV, iPod docking station, Nespresso coffee maker and a large marble bathroom. Specialist facilities include a dedicated executive floor, and a versatile range of conference and meeting rooms. The hotel offers 7 distinctive restaurants and bars, including a fine-dining Italian restaurant, a poolside bar and a bar offering panoramic views over the city. Recreation amenities include an extensive spa featuring separate male and female areas, an authentic Moroccan hammam, a state of the art health club, a 25m outdoor pool, and a children's pool.
Ferrari World and Abu Dhabi Corniche are just 10 minutes drive away, whilst Abu Dhabi International Airport can be reached in 15 minutes. The HILTON CAPITAL GRAND ABUDHABI is a Tourist, Art Decor, Business, Leisure, City hotel, Design Trendy, Full Service, Child Friendly hotel. Located in City Centre - Abu Dhabi area. Shopping is accessible by bus/taxi and the nightlife/restaurants are located in the hotel.
