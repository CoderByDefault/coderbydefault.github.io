Hotels in Saudi Arabia

Hotels in Makkah


Makkah Hilton Hotel

The Makkah Hilton is a five star luxury hotel set in the heart of Makkah. Accommodations feature city, mountain, Kaaba or Holy Haram Mosque views, and guests can pray with views over the Holy Haram in two air-conditioned and carpeted 10,000 seat prayer halls. Each room contains a desk and sofa, whilst suites feature a separate living room with dining area and executive rooms give access to the executive lounge. The hotel is home to 4 restaurants and a cafe serving traditional Arabian coffee. There is an extensive onsite shopping mall with 450 brand shops and a food court. Nearby attractions include the Kaaba and the ZamZam Well, with many other important holy sites less than 5 miles away. Please note that as per Saudi law, only Muslim guests can access the hotel and the Holy City of Makkah.
The MAKKAH HILTON HOTEL is a Tourist, Family Style, Modern, Leisure, City hotel, Full Service hotel. Located in Infront Of Haram area. Shopping is within walking distance and the nightlife/restaurants are easily accessible by taxi or bus from the hotel.

Al Mona Ajyad Hotel

Al Mona Ajyad is located about 800 metres from Al-Masjid Al-Haram in Makkah. Several shops and restaurants are within walking distance. There is also a restaurant inside the hotel and room service is provided. Free Wi-Fi is available. The rooms are air conditioned and with carpeted flooring. They include a satellite TV and a wardrobe. They also have a fridge and an electric kettle. The bathroom comes with a shower and free toiletries. The hotel has a spacious lobby and a 24-hour front desk, offering car rental and luggage storage. Laundry and ironing services are available as well. The airport is less than 90 minutes by car from Al Mona Ajyad. Free private parking is available on the hotel’s site

Hotels in Madinah



Madinah Marriott Hotel

As per Saudi law, only Muslim guests can access the hotel and the Holy City of Madinah. Marriott Hotel offers Luxurious Rooms with choice of a King Bed or two Double Beds with all major guest amenities. In an effort to make the stay more comfortable Hotel provides round the clock free shuttle buses to Prophet Mohammad's Mosque, a 2 minutes drive or just an 8 to 10 minutes healthy walk. Madinah Marriott Hotels staff will pamper you, the amenities, services and the citys peaceful atmosphere will leave you wishing to stay longer. It has easy access to major historical sites like Mount Ohud, Quba Mosque, Qiblatain Mosque, Baqi etc. Madinah Marriott Hotel provides an ideal accommodation for Hajj and Umrah pilgrims, visit during the fasting month of Ramadan, for Ziyarah or a Business Trip. Madinah is a Holy City and only Muslims with a Visa can stay in the limits of the Holy Mosque, including the Madinah Marriott Hotel.
The MADINAH MARRIOTT HOTEL is a Tourist, Family Style, Modern, City hotel hotel. Located in City Centre - King Faisal Street area. Shopping is accessible by bus/taxi and the nightlife/restaurants are easily accessible by taxi or bus from the hotel.

Riyadh Marriott Hotel

The Riyadh Marriott Hotel is a 5 star hotel located in the heart of the city's financial centre and governmental agencies. The hotel is just minutes from popular golf courses and shopping malls. Each of the air-conditioned rooms and suites feature coffee/tea making facility, iron/ironing board, satellite TV, direct telephone and a bathroom with shower. Featuring Mediterranean cuisine, the hotel restaurants offers breakfast, lunch and dinner while light snacks and refreshments are available at the lounge. In addition grilled beef cuts, chops and seafood are available at the in-house steakhouse. Various international restaurants are just nearby from the hotel. Leisure facilities include an indoor swimming pool, full service spa treatments, sauna, jacuzzi, tennis court and a fully equipped fitness centre. Riyadh King Khalid International Airport is 40-minutes drive away from the hotel.
The RIYADH MARRIOTT HOTEL is a Tourist hotel. Located in City Center - Al Mathar area. Shopping is accessible by bus/taxi and the nightlife/restaurants are located in the hotel.
