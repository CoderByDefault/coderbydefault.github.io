Bulgari Hotel Milano

This 5 star hotel is located in the city centre of Milan and was established in 2004. It is close to the Scala ,Duomo and the nearest station is Montenapolone. The Hotel has a restaurant, a bar, a conference room, an indoor swimming pool and a fitness centre/gym. All 58 rooms are equipped with minibar, hairdryer, safe, trouser press and air conditioning.
The BULGARI HOTEL MILANO is a Tourist hotel. Located in Orto Botanico area. Shopping is accessible by bus/taxi and the nightlife/restaurants are easily accessible by taxi or bus from the hotel.

Hotel Tiffany Milano

Hotel Tiffany Milano is an elegant and modern structure located close to Milan Rho Pero convention center and only 30 minutes from the center of the city. Offering airport and exhibition shuttles (at extra costs and upon reservation), the Tiffany Milano has a variety of business facilities including conference rooms and free internet access in all rooms. On site you will also find a restaurant and bar and a fitness center. The Tiffany is well-placed for visiting towns outside of Milan, such as Pavia and Vigevano, both home to medieval churches and historic buildings. Ticino Park is also close by and the hotel can organize rafting, canoeing, hiking or cycling excursions.
The TIFFANY MILANO is a Tourist hotel. Located in Milan area. Shopping is accessible by bus/taxi and the nightlife/restaurants are located in the hotel.

The Hotel Indigo Rome

The Hotel Indigo Rome - St. George is on the exclusive Via Giulia Street in Roma. Free Wi-Fi access is available throughout the property. The air-conditioned rooms include a flat-screen TV, an electric teakettle and a minibar. The private bathrooms have a hairdryer and free toiletries. The Hotel Indigo Rome features a rooftop bar and restaurant. The hotel is just over a quarter mile from Castel Sant Angelo. The Vatican City is a 10-minute walk away.
The HOTEL INDIGO ROME - ST.GEORGE is a Tourist hotel. Located in Historic Centre area. Shopping is within walking distance and the nightlife/restaurants are located in the hotel.

Holiday Inn Hotel Rome Pisana

The Holiday Inn Hotel Rome Pisana is a modern four star property located around 7km from the centre of Rome and the main tourist attractions. The hotel offers accommodation in a green residential area on the outskirts of the city and is just 5 minutes drive from Villa Pamphili, Rome's largest park. In-room amenities include en-suite bathrooms, air-conditioning, a flat-screen TV and complimentary WiFi access. The hotel is home to 2 restaurants serving a variety of local and international dishes, whilst the lounge bar provides an extensive range of wines and cocktails. Additional facilities include a well-equipped fitness centre and 7 meeting rooms featuring the latest in audio visual technologies. The hotel is around 10 minutes drive from the Vatican and Trastevere Train Station, and 20 minutes from Fiumicino International Airport. The HOLIDAY INN ROME PISANA (formerly JSH HOTEL ROME) is a Tourist, Modern, Business, City hotel, Airport Hotel hotel. Located in ROME area. Shopping is within walking distance and the nightlife/restaurants are easily accessible by taxi or bus from the hotel.
