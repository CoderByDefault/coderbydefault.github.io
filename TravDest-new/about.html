About Us

Who Are we



We would like to introduce TRAVEL DESTINATIONS., as an established Travel Management Company,
the Management and staff of Travel Destinations have been associated with the travel
industry for many years and are qualified, experienced and competent
to handle travel projects of any magnitude.


Why us



For the benefit of our patrons we also provide services for Umrah& Hajj, which specializes in the arrangements
of pilgrimage tour packages for individuals, families and groups to the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia.
We also provide complete packages for Inbound Tours of Pakistan, City Tours, specially
arranged for Groups, tailor made for families and individuals.


Travel Destinations provides best available rates for over 17,000 hotels around the world,
specializes in handling conferences and seminars besides offering host of ground
related services including sightseeing tours, car hire, and tailor made excursion packages,
furnished apartments and hotel accommodation globally.


Our speciality



Travel Destinations assist in visa facilitation for Dubai, Egypt, Saudi Arabia, China,
Vietnam, Hong Kong, Philippines, Sri Lanka, Malaysia, Thailand, Singapore,
Turkey, Tajikistan, Uzbekistan, Kyrgyzstan, Turkmenistan, Indonesia,
Bangladesh and many more.


We are desirous of handling the travel arrangements of your organization and will be pleased to
provide the following one-window services at all times.


  • Prepare itineraries
  • Domestic & International Reservations & Ticketing

  • Hotel Reservations, Rent-a-Car, in-Bound/out-bound and domestic tours facilities are available.
  • International prepaid hotel reservations in all stars categories in 660 cities

  • Delivery of tickets wherever required
  • Cheapest fare structures applicable on international routings
  • Tailor made tours- local and out of Pakistan
