USA Visa Requirements

  • 1 Photo size 50mm X 50mm, with a white back ground, non glossy paper and without any Cap or Hijaab.
  • A visa application form must be filled on web site: http://evisaforms.state.gov
  • Separate Visa form for all applicants.

    • Non Immigrant
    Supplemental
    Parents details

  • Visa Fees Rs. 12200/- Approx. (Inclusive of Amex charges w.e.f 21 OCT 2008)
  • At the time of Submission the applicant is not required to provide any supporting documents.

    • Note

  • No Manual DS-156 form is accepted, DS 157, DS-158 and parents information form has to be hand written.
  • Submission Timings: 09:00 a.m. to 12:30 p.m.
  • Delivery Timings: 02:00 p.m. to 04:30 p.m
  • The Embassy returns your passport with all attached documents after 12 working days for the interview.

    • The Visa Waiver Program (VWP) enables citizens of participating countries to travel to the U.S. for tourism or business for 90 days or less without obtaining a U.S. visa.
    Currently there are 27 participating countries in the VWP: Andorra, Austria, Australia, Belgium, Brunei, Denmark, Finland, France, Germany, Iceland, Ireland, Italy, Japan, Liechtenstein, Luxembourg, Monaco, The Netherlands, New Zealand, Norway, Portugal, San Marino, Singapore, Slovenia, Spain, Sweden, Switzerland and the United Kingdom.

    Required Information for Visa Processing

  • Home Address, Contact No. and email address
  • Business Telephone No. and Fax No
  • Spouse's Name and Date of Birth
  • Marital Status
  • Name, Telephone No. ( Home, Business and Cell) of person in U.S. who you will be staying with.
  • Have you been in the U.S? When and for how long?
  • Have you ever been refused a U.S. visa? When, Where, What type of visa?
  • Has anyone ever filled an immigrant visa petition on you behalf?
  • Do you have any immediate family in the U.S? Indicate that person's status in the U.S.
  • Supplemental Non-Immigrant visa application to be filled.
  • Applicant Parent's Information form to be filled.
