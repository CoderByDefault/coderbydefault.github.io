Berjaya Eden Park London Hotel

Berjaya Eden Park London Hotel is located in the heart of Bayswater, just a short walk to Bayswater and Queensway Tube Stations which provides easy access to the rest of the city and very close to Kensington Gardens and Hyde Park. This Victorian inspire hotel features 111 rooms furnished with television, in-room safe deposit, hairdryer, coffee/tea making facilities, work desk and private bathroom. Continental or full English breakfast is served daily and the onsite restaurant serves Malaysian and International dishes accompanied with international wine, beer, cocktails and non-alcoholic drinks. In addition, the hotel features traditional English bar which also offers light lunch and afternoon tea. Other facilities include 24-hour concierge, business center, laundry and valet services, room service and foreign currency exchange. The Oxford Street, Notting Hill and Westfield Shopping Centre are also within easy reach. London Heathrow Airport is approximately 14 miles from the hotel
The BERJAYA EDEN PARK LONDON HOTEL is a Tourist, Business, Boutique, City hotel, Child Friendly hotel. Located in Bayswater area. Shopping is accessible by bus/taxi and the nightlife/restaurants are located in the hotel.

Radisson Edwardian Grafton Hotel

The Radisson Edwardian Grafton Hotel, located in the West End, is perfectly situated for both business and leisure travelers. The mood at this central London hotel is smart, colonial and clubby. Public spaces and suites are filled with displays of eastern art, including Qing dynasty temple bells and new works by Vietnamese painters An Quan and Bich Nguyet. The 330 rooms and suites at the Grafton Hotel London are ergonomically designed to provide guests with a comfortable environment. Sleek furnishings, marble bathrooms, and crisp linen add to the luxury environment. Larger deluxe rooms include upgraded amenities, such as extra work space and seating area. A host of services and amenities are available at the Grafton Hotel. Key features include a business center and fitness room. Open for breakfast, lunch and dinner, the Aston Restaurant and Bar offers modern British cuisine, comprised of simple food made of quality ingredients.
The RADISSON BLU EDWARDIAN GRAFTON is a Tourist hotel. Located in City Centre - West End area. Shopping is accessible by bus/taxi and the nightlife/restaurants are easily accessible by taxi or bus from the hotel.

The Ramada Hotel Birmingham

The hotel is ideally positioned in the heart of the city centre, within the Mailbox complex, which enjoys a picturesque canal-side setting and which is full of designer shops, bars and restaurants. Ideal for both business and leisure, the hotel is close to the business area of the city and a vast array of nightlife and shopping options. The hotel hosts 90 en suite rooms and 3 meeting rooms with the largest seating up to 40 guests, as well as a bar, cafe, restaurant and lounge. Free wifi Internet access is available in all the public areas. The air-conditioned hotel houses a lobby, 24-hour reception and check-out service, a hotel safe and a cloakroom. Lift access and room service are also available. For an additional fee, guests can take advantage of the hotel's laundry service and car park. The Ramada Hotel Birmingham City Centre is a Tourist hotel. Located in Birmingham area. Shopping is within walking distance and the nightlife/restaurants are located in the hotel. This recently modernised and fully refurbished hotel is great for both leisure and business guests. It is in a handy location on the popular Hagley Road just three miles from Edgbaston and one mile from Broad Street the Bull Ring Shopping Centre and Birminghamas other city centre attractions bars restaurants clubs and theatres. This is a very comfortable and modern hotel in a great location for a short break to Birmingham or a business trip.
