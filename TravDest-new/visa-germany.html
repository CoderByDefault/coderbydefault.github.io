Germany Visa Requirements

  • Two application forms, completed and signed by applicant (including statement and undertaking)
  • Working and leave certificate
  • 3 recent passport size photographs (white background-non glossy paper)
  • Bank Statement of the last 3 months (Personal)
  • Proof of income (salary certificate)
  • AIG Insurance
  • Proof of sufficient financial means for the planned stay (travellers cheques, cash, credit cards)
  • Confirmed hotel reservation.
  • Ticket/ Flight reservations (booking slip)
  • Copy of CNIC
  • Previous passport(s), if any
  • Visa Fee: 6400/- Approx.
  • Personal Appearance- Not required for a passenger who has traveled within last 2 years minimum 2 times.
  • 1st time readable passport holders personal appearance required at the time of delivery for signing.
  • If are you applying in Islamabad, you are required to take an appointment. Also the application should be typed.

    • Business Visa Requirements

  • Two application forms, completed and signed by applicant (including statement and undertaking)
  • Covering letter from the company in Pakistan
  • Working certificate stating applicants position and duration of employment
  • Proof of income (salary certificates)

    • Sponsorship:

  • Official invitation from the German partner, stating duration and purpose of visit
  • (one copy of the invitation has to be send directly to the Consulate ex Germany) as well
  • b. Proof of business activities with the company in Germany
  • 3 recent passport size photographs (white background-non glossy paper)
  • Bank Statement of the last 6 months (Personal & Company)
  • Copies of NTN, Chamber's Certificate, Import Export License and Company Registration.
  • AIG Insurance
  • Proof of sufficient financial means for the planned stay (travellers cheques, cash, credit cards)
  • Confirmed hotel reservation.
  • Flight reservations
  • Copy of CNIC

    • Requirements

  • 1. Two application forms, completed and signed by applicant (including statement and undertaking)
  • 2. Three recent passport-size photographs not older than six months
  • 3. Passport, valid for at least three months beyond intended stay
  • 4. Previous passport(s), if any
  • 5. Photocopies of pages 1-4 of current passport and of all previous Schengen visas
  • 6. Covering letter from the company of the applicant in Pakistan
  • 7. Companies exhibiting their products: Proof of booking and payment of exhibition fees or
  • 8. For visitors: Invitation letter by an official exhibitor or/ and proof of entry-cardvoucher.
  • 9. Travel health insurance valid for the Schengen States and covering up to 30.000 of medical expenses
  • 10. Flight and hotel reservations (booking slip)
  • 11. Proof of sufficient financial means for the planned stay (traveller cheques, cash, International credit cards)

    • Please note

  • Processing time is of minimum 10 weekdays
  • All applicants have to appear personally, except frequent travellers for the last two (2) years.
  • Companies should send their employees in a group to apply for the visa.
  • Only complete applications are to be accepted.
  • The Consulate might ask for additional documents if found necessary.
  • Visa applications can be submitted weeks ahead. Dates of validity on the visa sticker will be
  • according to the dates as per application, reservations and travel health insurance.
  • Long-term visas can be issued only in exceptional cases.
  • Spouses accompanying their partners on a business trip/ to a fair have to fulfill the requirements as for a tourist.
  • Original passports can be withdrawn after submission of application for travels (except Schengen States) during the processing time.
  • No other charges except the visa fee are applicable. This requirement sheet is free of charge!!!!
  • Opening hours of the Visa section are: 8.30 a.m. to 11.00 a.m. for submission of applications
  • (Mondays-Fridays); 12.00 p.m. to 13.00 p.m. for collection of passports (all working days).

  • Cellular No.
  • Monthly Income
  • Date of Joining Employer / Start of Business
